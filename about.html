About Us

Hi! We're Marianne and Matt Gee, the founders of Gees Bees Honey Company!

We became beekeepers in 2009 when we discovered and rescued a colony of honeybees living in the wall of our home. Today, we produce our own pure raw wildflower honey and work with beekeepers across Canada to bring you premium honey varieties.

If you have any questions about our products, please don't hesitate to ask!

We're proud to bring you both local and
premium Canadian specialty honey.
